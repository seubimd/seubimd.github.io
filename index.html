About BiMD

During the long evolution progress, some organisms have developed unique mechanism in forming micro/nano structures and special structure-activity relationship. These features provide abundant prototypes for the design of artificial materials and devices. Meanwhile, the sophisticated and fast developing electronics and related technologies promote the material designing and device manufacturing both theoretically and experimentally. By combing the prototypes of living things with modern manufacturing techniques, the achievements of these two independent branche are fully utilized and integrated, to solve the problems in biomedical engineering.
